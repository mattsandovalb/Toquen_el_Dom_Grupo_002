Coders Music School

At Coders Music School we bring together the 2 great branches of creativity to create a unique space that combines sound with lines of code.

At Coders Music School we dedicate the space to creativity and the use of new technologies together with music and instruments. Our facilities have computers and instruments of all kinds for experimentation and carrying out the projects that our MusicCoders must carry out during the course.
Coders Music School is a multicultural space that is committed to plurality, diversity and the insertion of all our students. At Coders Music School you will find a safe and inclusive space where you can develop your creativity. Here we are all the same. What are you waiting for to join our great group? We will wait for you!
